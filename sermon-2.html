Breaking the Chains of Limitation

Apostle Poliano Jose Gomes Rodrigues • Jan 2026 • Prophetic
Watch on YouTube Download Notes

Sermon Summary

In this powerful message, Apostle Poliano teaches on how to identify and break the spiritual chains that limit our growth and destiny. Drawing from scripture, he reveals the keys to walking in total freedom and authority.

Key Scriptures

  • Galatians 5:1 - "It is for freedom that Christ has set us free. Stand firm, then, and do not let yourselves be burdened again by a yoke of slavery."
  • Isaiah 10:27 - "And the yoke will be destroyed because of the anointing oil."
  • Psalm 107:14 - "He brought them out of darkness and the deepest gloom and broke away their chains."

Learning Aid

  • Identify the root cause of limitation (fear, sin, generational patterns).
  • Apply the Blood of Jesus to break legal rights of the enemy.
  • Walk in the authority of the believer.
