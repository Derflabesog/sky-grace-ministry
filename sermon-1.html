Walking in the Glory

Apostle Poliano • Jan 11, 2026 • Sunday Service
Watch on YouTube Download Notes

Sermon Summary

Experience the tangible presence of God as we explore what it means to carry His glory in our daily lives. This message challenges believers to move beyond the ordinary and step into the supernatural.

Key Scriptures

  • 2 Corinthians 3:18 - "And we all, who with unveiled faces contemplate the Lord’s glory, are being transformed into his image with ever-increasing glory."
  • Exodus 33:18 - "Then Moses said, 'Now show me your glory.'"
  • John 17:22 - "I have given them the glory that you gave me, that they may be one as we are one."

Learning Aid

  • The Glory is the weight of God's presence.
  • We are carriers of the Glory in earthen vessels.
  • Holiness is a prerequisite for sustaining the Glory.
