Who We Are

Sky Grace Ministry is a Christ-centered ministry committed to worship, prayer, discipleship, and community impact. We believe the Word of God transforms lives and brings direction for everyday living.

  • Christ-focused worship & prayer
  • Practical Bible teaching
  • Discipleship and spiritual growth
  • Family-friendly community

Core Values

These values guide how we serve God and people:

  • Faith — trusting God fully
  • Love — serving with compassion
  • Integrity — truth in word and action
  • Excellence — doing God’s work with honor
  • Service — ministry that meets real needs
