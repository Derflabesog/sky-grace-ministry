Cookie Policy

Transparent stewardship of your digital presence.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a smoother experience. They allow us to "remember" your choices, such as your preferred language or your login status on the Admin Dashboard.

2. Essential Cookies

These are strictly necessary for the website to function. We use them for:

  • Maintaining your secure session in the Admin Portal.
  • Remembering if you prefer English, Oshiwambo, or Afrikaans.

3. Managing Your Cookies

Most browsers allow you to block cookies through their settings. However, please note that if you disable essential cookies, some parts of the Sky Grace website (like the Language Selector) may not function correctly.
