Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including when you join our newsletter, submit a testimony, fill out a new member form, or make a donation. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and branch location.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for ministry purposes: to send the Weekly Revelation blog, provide updates on conferences, and manage your volunteer applications. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Financial Security

All donations are processed through secure, encrypted third-party payment gateways. Sky Grace Jesus Ministry does not store your credit card or bank account details on our servers.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your language preferences (English, Oshiwambo, etc.) and analyzing site traffic through our admin dashboard.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding your data or wish to be removed from our directory, please contact the Administration Office at admin@skygrace.com.
